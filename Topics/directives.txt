Angular Directives:
  In Angular, Directives are defined as classes that can add new behavior or modify existing behavior on an elements in the template.
  The main purpose of directives in Angular is to maneuver the DOM, by adding new elements to DOM, removing existing elements from the DOM
  and changing the appearance of the DOM elements.

Types of Directives-
  - Component directives
  - Structural directives
  - Attribute directives
